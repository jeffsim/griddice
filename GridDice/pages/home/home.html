1. The object of Grid Dice is to get the highest total score by placing dice in score boxes. The game ends when you have filled all score boxes.
2. To play, drag your finger/mouse across five dice in the grid, and then tap on a score box.

3. Scores follow the classic Yahtzee scoring rules.
Score box Scoring
Ones Sum of all one dice
Twos Sum of all two dice
Threes Sum of all three dice
Fours Sum of all four dice
Fives Sum of all five dice
Sixes Sum of all six dice
Bonus If the sum of the Ones through Sixes is 63, then 35 points are awarded
Three of a kind Sum of all dice
Four of a kind Sum of all dice
Full house 25 points
Small straight 30 points
Large straight 40 points
Five of a kind 50 points
Chance Sum of all dice
